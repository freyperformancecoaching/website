About Hayden Frey

Where rehab knowledge meets performance coaching.

Frey Performance was built for endurance athletes who want a smarter, more sustainable path to improvement. The goal is not just more miles or harder workouts. It is better decisions, better movement, and better outcomes over time.

The approach

Performance should feel informed, not random.

Hayden Frey is a Doctor of Physical Therapy, Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist, and endurance coach who works at the intersection of rehab, strength, and sport performance.

That background shapes every plan. Instead of treating strength, durability, mobility, and endurance as separate lanes, Frey Performance brings them together into one coaching process that makes sense for the whole athlete.

Whether you are returning from injury, preparing for your next race cycle, or trying to train more consistently, the focus stays the same: build a stronger engine without ignoring the body that has to carry it.
